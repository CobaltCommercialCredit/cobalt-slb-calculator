California Property Tax Reassessment (Prop 13)

$
%
MetricSale-LeasebackConventional Debt
A sale triggers a change-of-ownership reassessment under Prop 13, resetting the property tax basis to current market value. Under a NNN lease structure, that new tax bill passes through to the seller as tenant — this cost is specific to the SLB path and does not apply under the loan/retained-ownership alternative, since no sale occurs. If the lease is Gross/Modified Gross, some or all of this cost lands on the landlord instead and would be priced into the rent rather than billed separately — check the actual lease before treating this as a pass-through cost.
